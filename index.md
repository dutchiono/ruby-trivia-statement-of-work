# Ruby Trivia — Statement of Work & What I'm Owed

*Dutch (`dutchiono`). Everything below is from the record — my GitHub commits and the team's own messages, with dates. Nothing invented.*

## 1. The deal we agreed to — in writing, June 18 2026
Posted and pinned by Odi:

- **Odi (pinned, Jun 18):** *"…we have you down for **7.5% of the total generated in the first week. The 7.5% is for all the work you've done so far.** … when we have funds we will **contract with you for work, full hourly rate, we pay for work done. Happy to even pay 50% upfront.**"*

The hourly rate, set minutes later:

- **Odi:** *"btw what is your hour rate for ruby work after launch?"*
- **Odi:** ***"lets say $50/hr"***
- **Me:** *"That would be pretty damn comfortable for me for a long time."*
- **Me, accepting:** *"Fair is fair."*

**In plain terms:** 7.5% of first-week fees for everything up to launch, then **$50/hr** for work after — 50% upfront. No equity (left open to discuss).

## 2. The work I delivered
**106 hours** across **13 active build days** (May 29 – June 26), every day backed by the commit log. Built and shipped:

- **Trivia PWA** — daily quiz, practice, friend challenges, leaderboards, streaks, ranks/badges, 4 languages, installable.
- **Live multiplayer** — realtime WebSocket lobbies, matchmaking, graceful reconnect.
- **Backend & API** — Hono/Bun server, auth/registration, game logic, SQLite → MariaDB migration + SQL translation.
- **Web3 holder layer** — Solana wallet connect, holder verification, perks (multiplier, holders-only leaderboard, cosmetics).
- **On-chain / contracts**, **Telegram bot gateway**, **production infrastructure**, **app-store repackaging**, **token-launch support**, **educational variant**.

- **$350 for 106 hours** + a two-day, no-sleep launch = **$3.30 / hr.** Their **$50** valuation works out to **47¢ / hr.**
